A short, tender game about saying goodbye to a pet. It doesn't try to tell the
whole story of a life — instead it holds a few small, ordinary moments: a
favourite spot by the window, a walk you took a hundred times, the corner where
they always waited for you.

You move between these places at your own pace and stay with each one for as long
as you need. Nothing hurries you and nothing is undone; the game simply gives the
goodbye some room, and trusts you to know when you're ready to move on.
