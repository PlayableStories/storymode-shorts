A tiny game about slowing down. There's nothing to win, nowhere to be, and no
timer counting anything — just a small, unhurried space that quietly asks you to
take your time.

Nudge it gently and it answers a beat later than you expect. That pause is
deliberate: the game isn't waiting for you to *do* something, it's making a
little room for you to stop. A few minutes here is meant to feel less like
playing and more like exhaling.
